Privacy Policy

Effective:

Seldom Rest Innovations LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds educational and productivity software, including the ePiggy app. This policy describes how we handle personal information across our websites and consumer apps.

  • Account and contact information (e.g., name, email) when you sign up or contact support.
  • App usage data and device information for analytics and to improve performance.
  • Child user data (for ePiggy) limited to what is necessary to provide the service, with parent/guardian control.

How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share with service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, email), under contracts that require appropriate safeguards.

Children’s privacy

For apps like ePiggy used by children under 13, a parent/guardian account is required to create and manage child profiles. We collect only what is necessary to operate the service and provide parental controls. Please contact us to access or delete a child’s data.

Data security & retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above unless a longer period is required by law.

International users

We are a U.S. company. Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate.

Your choices & rights

  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Parents can manage and request deletion of a child’s profile at any time.
